But for those travelling abroad, somethings should be kept in mind: Travel advice & advisories! (ALSO, save your embassy's emergency-assistance to nationals 24/7-hotline on your phone!) While some government's write-ups make general rules, 2 actually make heat-maps that show you were the safe(r) & (more) dangerous parts of your destination are: →Australia 🇦🇺's & →the U.K. 🇬🇧's.
