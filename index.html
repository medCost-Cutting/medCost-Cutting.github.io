Welcome to the Self-Pay Healthcare Market!

Back in 2014, Mr. Parnell published the book, The Self-Pay Patient: Affordable Healthcare Choices in the Age of Obamacare (S.P.P.). And since books get outdated on printing, he also setup and managed & the now-late, great (just-cited) S.P.P. blog-edition, where people could share their own cost-cutting discoveries & how it worked for them. These would be added, and the listing ("The Self-Pay Healthcare Market") would become even MORE comprehensive!

This ever-improving menu continued developing and expanding… until 2021-2022. Then, it (unfortunately) vanished.

With medical-costs where they are now, would't it be great we had an updated list for now (2025 & beyond)?

Well, this is my attempt.

As can be imagined, there's A LOT of back-tracking to do: an impressive (& daunting) 296 options, if my count is correct. Of course, that won't stop me from adding my own discoveries, too.
